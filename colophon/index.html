Colophon

Akademický theme for Jekyll, created by Eric M. Fink, based on al-folio, with elements from tufte-jekyll (side/margin notes and figures in the style of Edward Tufte) and bijou (table and button styles).

Set in Portada and Adelle Sans by Veronika Burian & José Scaglione, and LFT Etica Mono by Leftloft.
